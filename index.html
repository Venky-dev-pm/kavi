FAQ

Questions & answers

Is KAVI really 100% local?
Yes. All AI processing — voice recognition, language understanding, and text-to-speech — runs entirely on your CPU. The only network traffic is optional OAuth for Google/Outlook calendar sync. Nothing else ever leaves your machine.
What are the system requirements?
Any modern laptop with 4GB+ RAM. No GPU required — KAVI uses lightweight AI models optimized for CPU inference. Total memory stays under 1.5GB. Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
How does transcription work without internet?
KAVI bundles a lightweight speech recognition model that runs entirely on your CPU. Audio is processed in smart segments for accuracy, transcribed locally, then passed to the AI for summarization. No internet connection needed for any of this.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
The Free plan gives you up to 10 meetings per week with full AI transcription, summaries, action items, search, Kanban board, and calendar sync. Pro ($7/month) unlocks unlimited meetings, automated daily/weekly reports, email dispatch, voice interface, and priority support.
